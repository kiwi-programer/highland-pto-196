Our Purpose

Driven by Vision,
Guided by Mission

The Highland PTO was started in the Fall of 2022 when Highland Elementary transitioned from a Site Council to a more inclusive PTO model — opening the door for greater parent and guardian participation than ever before.

The PTO's mission is to facilitate the betterment of Highland families, staff, and students.

  • Creating an open, welcoming and supportive learning environment
  • Fostering pride in the school and Scottie community
  • Developing and strengthening the community of advocates
  • Offering talent and financial assistance where needed
PTO Vision

The primary vision of the Highland PTO is to involve families and community members to enhance the students' education. The PTO serves as a communication liaison between the school and the family/guardian community, and as a fundraising organization for the school.
